Brain Computer Interface

Cerebral palsy (CP) is a group of disorders that affect movement and posture that has no known direct treatments. Our P300-based Brain Computer Interface proposes an approach to apply EEG-BCI intergrated robotic systems to enable communication and control for people with CP.
